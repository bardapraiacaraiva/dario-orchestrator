### Stage 1: $0 to $10K/month (The Founder-Led Stage)

**Goal**: Prove the business works. Get to product-market fit. Survive.

**Mindset**: Do things that don't scale (Paul Graham). Be the product, sales, and support.

| Area | Focus | Actions |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Product/Service** | Nail the offer, iterate based on feedback | Deliver personally, collect testimonials |
| **Sales** | Founder-led, 1:1 outreach, warm network | 50 conversations/week, close deals personally |
| **Marketing** | Organic content + warm outreach | 1 platform, daily content, DMs |
| **Operations** | Manual everything, document as you go | Google Sheets, basic tools, write SOPs as you work |
| **Finance** | Track revenue and expenses weekly | Simple spreadsheet, separate bank account |
| **Hiring** | Do NOT hire (yet) | Use freelancers for specific tasks only |

**Key Milestones:**
- [ ] First paying customer
- [ ] First 10 customers
- [ ] First $1K month
- [ ] First $5K month
- [ ] First $10K month
- [ ] 3 consecutive $10K+ months (consistency = real)

**Stage 1 Systems:**
| System | Tool | Purpose |
|--------|------|---------|
| CRM | Notion / Google Sheets | Track leads and customers |
| Email | Mailchimp / ConvertKit free | Email list and sequences |
| Payments | Stripe / PayPal | Accept payments |
| Project | Todoist / Notion | Task management |
| Content | Canva + scheduling tool | Social media |

**Common Mistakes at This Stage:**
- Hiring too early (cash drain before PMF)
- Building complex tech before validating demand
- Spending on ads before organic proves the offer works
- Perfectionism (ship fast, iterate faster)
- Not charging enough (underpricing kills businesses)

### Stage 2: $10K to $100K/month (The Systems Stage)

**Goal**: Remove yourself as the bottleneck. Build systems. Scale what works.

**Mindset**: You are no longer the product. Build the machine that delivers the product.

| Area | Focus | Actions |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Product/Service** | Standardize delivery, create SOPs | Document every process, create templates |
| **Sales** | Build a repeatable sales process | Sales script, CRM pipeline, follow-up sequences |
| **Marketing** | Scale proven channels, add paid | 2-3 platforms, content system, first ad campaigns |
| **Operations** | First hires, delegation framework | VA, specialist contractors, project management |
| **Finance** | Monthly P&L, cash flow management | Accounting software, quarterly review |
| **Hiring** | First 2-5 team members | Hire for weaknesses, train for culture |

**Hiring Triggers (When to Hire):**

| Trigger | Role to Hire | Type |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Founder doing admin > 10hrs/week | Virtual Assistant | Contractor |
| Sales pipeline has > 20 qualified leads/week | Sales rep / Closer | Contractor or FT |
| Content creation taking > 15hrs/week | Content creator | Contractor |
| Customer support > 10hrs/week | Support specialist | Contractor |
| Technical work blocking growth | Developer / Specialist | Contractor or FT |
| Financial complexity increasing | Bookkeeper | Contractor |

**Delegation Framework (Gerber E-Myth):**

| Task Category | Founder Does? | Delegate To | Priority |
|---------------|--------------|-------------|----------|
| $10/hr tasks (admin, data entry, scheduling) | NO | VA | Immediate |
| $50/hr tasks (support, basic marketing) | NO | Specialist | Month 1-2 |
| $100/hr tasks (sales, content strategy) | MAYBE | Trained team | Month 3-6 |
| $500/hr tasks (strategy, partnerships, product) | YES | Nobody yet | Keep |
| $1000/hr tasks (vision, culture, big deals) | YES | Nobody ever | Keep |

**Stage 2 Systems:**
| System | Tool | Purpose |
|--------|------|---------|
| CRM | HubSpot / Pipedrive | Sales pipeline |
| Email marketing | ActiveCampaign / ConvertKit | Automation |
| Project management | Asana / ClickUp | Team coordination |
| SOPs | Notion / Loom | Process documentation |
| Accounting | QuickBooks / Xero | Financial management |
| Analytics | Google Analytics + Mixpanel | Data-driven decisions |
| Communication | Slack + Zoom | Team collaboration |

### Stage 3: $100K to $1M/month (The Leadership Stage)

**Goal**: Build the organization. Create leverage. Think like a CEO, not an operator.

**Mindset**: Your job is to build the team that builds the business. Focus on culture, vision, and strategy.

| Area | Focus | Actions |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Product/Service** | Product-market expansion, new offerings | R&D, customer advisory board, product roadmap |
| **Sales** | Sales team, multiple channels | Sales manager, SDRs, channel partnerships |
| **Marketing** | Full marketing team, brand building | CMO/head of marketing, multi-channel campaigns |
| **Operations** | COO-level thinking, process optimization | Operations manager, automation, efficiency |
| **Finance** | CFO-level, strategic finance | CFO or fractional CFO, budgeting, forecasting |
| **Hiring** | Management layer, culture guardians | Department heads, HR processes, performance reviews |

**Organizational Chart Evolution:**

```
$0-10K: Founder does everything

$10K-100K:
  Founder (CEO/Sales/Strategy)
  ├── VA (Admin/Support)
  ├── Contractor (Marketing)
  └── Contractor (Delivery)

$100K-1M:
  Founder (CEO)
  ├── Head of Sales
  │   ├── SDR
  │   └── Closer
  ├── Head of Marketing
  │   ├── Content Creator
  │   └── Ads Manager
  ├── Head of Operations
  │   ├── Delivery Team
  │   └── Support Team
  └── Finance (fractional CFO)
```

**EOS Implementation (Wickman):**

| Component | Description | Action |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **Vision** | Where are we going? | V/TO (Vision/Traction Organizer) |
| **People** | Right people, right seats | Accountability chart + People Analyzer |
| **Data** | Scorecard with 5-15 weekly metrics | Weekly scorecard review |
| **Issues** | Identify, Discuss, Solve | Weekly L10 meetings |
| **Process** | Document core processes | 3-7 core processes, SOPs |
| **Traction** | Quarterly Rocks (priorities) | 3-7 rocks per quarter |

**Stage 3 Systems:**
| System | Tool | Purpose |
|--------|------|---------|
| ERP/All-in-one | HubSpot Enterprise / Salesforce | Full business ops |
| HR | Gusto / BambooHR | People management |
| BI/Analytics | Tableau / Looker / Metabase | Business intelligence |
| Knowledge base | Notion / Confluence | Company wiki |
| Legal | Ironclad / DocuSign | Contracts, compliance |

### Revenue Milestone Checklist

**$0 → $10K/mo:**
- [ ] Product-market fit confirmed (retention > 80%)
- [ ] Repeatable sales process (can close without heroics)
- [ ] Positive unit economics (LTV > 3x CAC)
- [ ] 3 consecutive months at $10K+
- [ ] First SOPs documented

**$10K → $100K/mo:**
- [ ] Team of 3-5 (at least 2 full-time equivalent)
- [ ] 80% of $10/hr tasks delegated
- [ ] Paid acquisition channel with positive ROAS
- [ ] Monthly financial review process
- [ ] Customer satisfaction system (NPS/CSAT)
- [ ] First management hire (department lead)

**$100K → $1M/mo:**
- [ ] Management team in place (3+ leaders)
- [ ] Founder works ON business 80%+ of time
- [ ] Multiple revenue streams or products
- [ ] Annual planning process (EOS or similar)
- [ ] Company culture documented and reinforced
- [ ] Board or advisory board in place
- [ ] Legal structure optimized for scale

### Scaling Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Stage | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| **Cash crunch from growth** | $10K-100K | Maintain 3-6 month runway, manage receivables |
| **Quality drops with scale** | $10K-100K | SOPs, QA checkpoints, customer feedback loops |
| **Founder burnout** | All | Delegate early, protect energy, hire for weaknesses |
| **Key person dependency** | $10K-100K | Document everything, cross-train, no single points of failure |
| **Culture erosion** | $100K-1M | Define values early, hire for culture fit, fire fast for misfit |
| **Market shift** | All | Stay close to customers, diversify, watch trends |
| **Bad hires** | $10K-100K | Structured interviews, trial periods, reference checks |
| **Over-investment in one channel** | All | 30/30/40 rule (no channel > 40% of revenue) |

## Red Flags

Stop and warn the user if:
- Trying to hire before $10K/month (too early, burn cash)
- No SOPs at $50K/month (scaling chaos)
- Founder still doing $10/hr tasks at $100K/month (bottleneck)
- No financial tracking at any stage (flying blind)
- Hiring friends/family instead of qualified people (emotional, not strategic)
- Scaling a business with negative unit economics (scaling losses)
- Zero delegation plan (founder will burn out)
- Adding complexity (new products, new markets) before mastering current model
- No management layer at $100K+ (span of control issues)
- Growing team faster than revenue (cash crisis incoming)
