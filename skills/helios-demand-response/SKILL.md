# HELIOS-DEMAND-RESPONSE

## Programs típicos
- **Capacity DR:** dispatch during peak hours
- **Economic DR:** dispatch when prices spike (PLD)
- **Ancillary DR:** frequency regulation
- **Emergency DR:** grid emergency events
- **Time-of-Use:** behavioral DR

## VPP (Virtual Power Plant)
- Aggregation de DERs (Distributed Energy Resources)
- Bid into wholesale market collectively
- Examples: Tesla VPP (Australia), Octopus Energy (UK)
- BR: emerging, no formal program yet

## DERMS (DER Management System)
- Software para orchestrate DERs
- Real-time visibility + control
- Stack: GE, Schneider, AutoGrid, Smarter Grid Solutions
