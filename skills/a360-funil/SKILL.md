## Workflow

### Step 1: Value Ladder Design (Brunson)

Map the complete ascension path:

```
                                    ┌─────────────────┐
                                    │ HIGH-TICKET      │  $5K-50K+
                                    │ (Done-For-You)   │  1:1, custom, VIP
                                    ├─────────────────┤
                               ┌────│ CORE OFFER       │  $200-2000
                               │    │ (Main product)   │  Course, program, service
                               │    ├─────────────────┤
                          ┌────│────│ TRIPWIRE         │  $7-47
                          │    │    │ (Low-ticket)     │  Mini-course, template, trial
                          │    │    ├─────────────────┤
                     ┌────│────│────│ LEAD MAGNET      │  FREE
                     │    │    │    │ (Value exchange) │  Guide, checklist, webinar
                     │    │    │    └─────────────────┘
                     ▼    ▼    ▼           ▼
              [Cold Traffic → Warm Lead → Customer → Superfan]
```

### Step 2: Lead Magnet Design (Hormozi)

**The 7 Lead Magnet Types** (ranked by effectiveness):

| Type | Example | Effort to Create | Conversion |
|------|---------|-------------------|------------|
| **Free trial / Freemium** | 7-day access, free tier | High | Very High |
| **Tool / Calculator** | ROI calculator, assessment | High | Very High |
| **Checklist / Cheat sheet** | "10-Point Audit Checklist" | Low | High |
| **Template / Swipe file** | Email templates, scripts | Low | High |
| **Mini-course / Video series** | 3-part video training | Medium | High |
| **Webinar / Workshop** | Live or recorded session | Medium | Medium-High |
| **eBook / Guide** | "Complete Guide to X" | Medium | Medium |

**Lead Magnet Quality Criteria:**
- [ ] Solves ONE specific problem (not everything)
- [ ] Delivers quick win in < 10 minutes
- [ ] Naturally leads to core offer (solves WHAT, core offer solves HOW)
- [ ] Has a compelling name with clear benefit
- [ ] Easy to consume (< 3000 words / < 15 min video)

**Lead Magnet Name Formula:**
"The [ADJECTIVE] [FORMAT] to [SPECIFIC RESULT] in [TIMEFRAME]"
Example: "The 5-Minute SEO Audit Checklist to Find Hidden Traffic Leaks"

### Step 3: Funnel Type Selection (Brunson)

| Funnel Type | Best For | Pages | Complexity |
|-------------|----------|-------|------------|
| **Lead Magnet Funnel** | List building | Opt-in → Thank You | Simple |
| **Tripwire Funnel** | Self-liquidating offers | Opt-in → Sales → OTO → Thank You | Medium |
| **Webinar Funnel** | Mid-high ticket | Register → Webinar → Application/Checkout | Medium |
| **Challenge Funnel** | Community, courses | Register → 5-day content → Offer | Medium |
| **Application Funnel** | High-ticket services | Opt-in → Video → Application → Call | Medium |
| **VSL Funnel** | Any price point | Video Sales Letter → Checkout | Simple |
| **Book Funnel** | Authority building | Free+Shipping → OTO → OTO2 | Medium |
| **Summit Funnel** | List building at scale | Register → Sessions → All-Access Pass | Complex |

### Step 4: Funnel Page Blueprints

**Opt-in Page (Lead Magnet):**
1. Headline: [Specific promise + timeframe]
2. Sub-headline: "For [avatar] who wants [outcome]"
3. 3-5 bullet points of what they'll learn/get
4. Email capture form (name + email minimum)
5. Social proof: "Join X+ [professionals/entrepreneurs/etc.]"
6. Image of lead magnet (mockup)

**Sales Page (Core Offer):**
1. Hook: Problem agitation headline
2. Story: Epiphany Bridge narrative (Brunson)
3. Offer: Full stack with bonuses (from a360-oferta)
4. Social proof: Testimonials, case studies, logos
5. FAQ: Address top 5-7 objections
6. Guarantee: Risk reversal
7. CTA: Clear, repeated 3-4 times throughout page
8. Urgency: Deadline or scarcity element

### Step 5: Traffic Strategy (Hormozi 4 Core)

| Method | Type | Cost | Time to Results | Scalability |
|--------|------|------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Warm Outreach** | Free | $0 | Immediate | Low (limited by network) |
| **Content (organic)** | Free | $0 | 30-90 days | High (compounds) |
| **Cold Outreach** | Free | $0 | 1-2 weeks | Medium |
| **Paid Ads** | Paid | $5-100+/day | 3-7 days | Very High |

**Phase 1 (Day 0-30)**: Warm outreach + content
**Phase 2 (Day 30-60)**: Add cold outreach + first paid ads
**Phase 3 (Day 60-90)**: Scale paid ads + content flywheel

**Dream 100 Strategy (Chet Holmes):**
1. List 100 people/platforms where your avatar congregates
2. Engage daily (comment, share, collaborate)
3. Propose collaboration (podcast, guest post, JV)
4. Convert their audience into your funnel

### Step 6: Email Sequences

| Sequence | Trigger | Length | Goal |
|----------|---------|--------|------|
| **Welcome** | Lead magnet opt-in | 5-7 emails | Deliver value, introduce brand |
| **Indoctrination** | After welcome | 3-5 emails | Build belief, share story |
| **Soap Opera** (Brunson) | Post-indoctrination | 5 emails | Create emotional connection, sell |
| **Abandoned Cart** | Cart visit, no purchase | 3-5 emails | Recover lost sales |
| **Post-Purchase** | After buying | 3-5 emails | Onboard, prevent refunds, upsell |
| **Re-engagement** | 30+ days inactive | 3 emails | Win back or clean list |

| **Upsell/OTO** | Take rate | 10% | 20% | 35%+ |

### Step 8: Funnel Math

Calculate the economics:

```
Monthly traffic needed = Revenue goal / (visitors → customer conversion rate x AOV)

Example:
- Revenue goal: $10,000/mo
- Funnel conversion: 2% visitors → customers
- AOV: $200
- Traffic needed: $10,000 / (0.02 x $200) = 2,500 visitors/mo

If CPC = $1.50:
- Ad budget needed: 2,500 x $1.50 = $3,750/mo
- ROI: $10,000 / $3,750 = 2.67x (profitable)
```

## Red Flags

Stop and warn the user if:
- No lead magnet (going straight to paid offer = expensive)
- Lead magnet solves the entire problem (no reason to buy core offer)
- Funnel has more than 5 steps before purchase (too much friction)
- No email sequence after opt-in (leads go cold in 48h)
- Relying 100% on paid traffic with unproven funnel (burn cash fast)
- No retargeting plan for visitors who don't convert
- Funnel math shows negative ROI at current conversion rates
- Tripwire price is higher than core offer trial (inverted value ladder)
- No mobile optimization for funnel pages (60%+ traffic is mobile)
