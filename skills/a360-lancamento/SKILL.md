## Workflow

### Step 0: Launch Type Selection

| Launch Type | Audience Size | Budget | Complexity | Best For |
|-------------|--------------|--------|------------|----------|
| **Seed Launch** (Walker) | 0-100 people | $0-100 | Low | First launch, validation |
| **Internal Launch** (Walker) | Existing list | $100-500 | Medium | List with 500+ subscribers |
| **JV Launch** (Walker) | Partners' lists | $500-5000 | High | Established product, affiliate model |
| **Evergreen Launch** | Automated funnel | $500-2000 | Medium | After live launch proven |
| **Challenge Launch** (Brunson) | Any | $200-1000 | Medium | Courses, programs, coaching |
| **Webinar Launch** (Brunson) | Any | $300-2000 | Medium | B2B, consulting, SaaS |

### Phase 1: PRE-LAUNCH (Days -30 to -1)

#### Week 1-2: Seed & Build Foundation (Days -30 to -14)

**Day -30 to -21: Audience Building**
- [ ] Identify 5 congregations where avatar hangs out (from a360-avatar)
- [ ] Begin daily content on primary platform (value, not selling)
- [ ] Launch lead magnet to start capturing emails
- [ ] Personal outreach to 50 potential customers (Hormozi warm outreach)
- [ ] Set up email service provider + segmentation

**Day -20 to -14: Seed Launch Content (Walker PLC)**

PLC = Pre-Launch Content (3-piece sideways sales letter)

| PLC | Theme | Content | Goal |
|-----|-------|---------|------|
| **PLC 1** | The Opportunity | "Here's what's possible..." | Create curiosity, establish authority |
| **PLC 2** | The Transformation | "Here's how it works..." | Teach framework, build trust |
| **PLC 3** | The Experience | "Here's what happens when..." | Social proof, case studies, desire |

Each PLC should be:
- Video (5-15 min) OR long-form email/post
- End with a question to drive engagement
- Include a call to join the waitlist

#### Week 3: Build Anticipation (Days -14 to -7)

- [ ] Publish PLC 1 — The Opportunity
- [ ] Send email sequence teasing the upcoming launch
- [ ] Share behind-the-scenes content (humanizes the process)
- [ ] Collect waitlist signups (target: 100+ for Seed, 500+ for Internal)
- [ ] Create FOMO: "founding member pricing for the first X people"
- [ ] Publish PLC 2 — The Transformation

#### Week 4: Final Countdown (Days -7 to -1)

- [ ] Publish PLC 3 — The Experience
- [ ] Send "cart opens in X days" emails (daily)
- [ ] Host a live Q&A or webinar (warm up the audience)
- [ ] Prep all sales assets: sales page, checkout, emails, ads
- [ ] Test ALL tech: payment processing, email delivery, access provisioning
- [ ] Brief any affiliates/partners on launch details
- [ ] Create urgency: "founding member pricing expires on launch day + 7"

### Phase 2: LAUNCH WEEK (Days 0 to 7)

#### Day 0: Open Cart

- [ ] Send "doors are open" email at 9:00 AM
- [ ] Publish announcement post on all social channels
- [ ] Go live (video/story) to announce launch
- [ ] DM top 20 warm prospects personally
- [ ] Activate paid ads (if budget allows)
- [ ] Monitor: sales, questions, objections in real-time

**Email Sequence (Launch Week):**

| Day | Email | Subject Line Theme | CTA |
|-----|-------|--------------------|-----|
| 0 | Open Cart | "It's here: [OFFER NAME]" | Buy now |
| 1 | Story/Transformation | "[Name] went from X to Y" | Buy now |
| 2 | FAQ / Objection Handler | "Your top questions answered" | Buy now |
| 3 | Social Proof | "Look what [customer] said..." | Buy now |
| 4 | Behind-the-Scenes | "Why I created this" (Epiphany Bridge) | Buy now |
| 5 | Urgency | "48 hours left at this price" | Buy now |
| 6 | Final Push | "Last chance — [specific scarcity]" | Buy now |
| 7 | Cart Close | "Doors closing at midnight" | Last chance |

#### Daily Launch Week Tasks

- [ ] Send scheduled email (morning)
- [ ] Post social content (midday)
- [ ] Respond to DMs, comments, questions (ongoing)
- [ ] Share new testimonials/wins as they come in
- [ ] Track conversions hourly on launch day and close day
- [ ] Address objections publicly (FAQ, live, stories)

### Phase 3: POST-LAUNCH (Days 8 to 30)

#### Week 1 Post-Launch (Days 8-14)

- [ ] Send "thank you" email to buyers with onboarding details
- [ ] Send "doors closed" email to non-buyers (with waitlist for next time)
- [ ] Deliver exceptional onboarding experience (first impression matters)
- [ ] Collect first testimonials (ask within 48h of purchase)
- [ ] Run post-mortem analysis (what worked, what didn't)

#### Week 2-4 Post-Launch (Days 15-30)

- [ ] Survey buyers: "What made you buy? What almost stopped you?"
- [ ] Survey non-buyers: "What held you back?"
- [ ] Gather and publish testimonials/case studies
- [ ] Iterate offer based on feedback
- [ ] Plan next launch or transition to evergreen funnel
- [ ] Calculate final launch metrics (see Step below)

### Walker's Launch Debrief Questions

1. What was the total revenue?
2. How many units sold?
3. What was the conversion rate?
4. Which PLC got the most engagement?
5. What was the #1 objection?
6. What was the most effective email?
7. What would I do differently next time?
8. Is this ready for an evergreen funnel?

## Red Flags

Stop and warn the user if:
- Launching with zero audience (no email list, no social following, no network)
- No lead magnet or waitlist built before launch day
- Skipping PLC sequence and going straight to "buy my thing"
- Tech not tested (broken checkout = lost sales permanently)
- No guarantee in the offer (conversion killer)
- Planning to launch and "see what happens" with no follow-up sequence
- Launch emails are features-focused instead of transformation-focused
- No post-launch debrief planned (repeating mistakes)
- Expecting 6-figure launch from a 200-person email list (unrealistic math)
- Using fake urgency ("only 3 spots left" when there's no limit)
