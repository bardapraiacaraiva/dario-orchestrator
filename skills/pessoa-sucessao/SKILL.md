## Workflow

### Step 1: Critical Role Identification

**Identify roles where vacancy creates maximum risk:**

| Role | Department | Incumbent | Criticality | Vacancy Risk | Coverage |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| [Role 1] | [Dept] | [Name] | Critical / High / Medium | High / Med / Low | [Successor?] |
| [Role 2] | [Dept] | [Name] | | | |

**Criticality Criteria:**
- **Critical**: Vacancy would halt operations, revenue loss, or compliance failure
- **High**: Significant disruption, 3+ months to fill, specialized knowledge
- **Medium**: Manageable disruption, role can be filled within reasonable time

**Risk Factors:**
- Retirement within 3 years
- Single incumbent with no backup
- Highly specialized knowledge not documented
- Market scarcity for this skill set
- History of short tenure in this role

### Step 3: High-Potential (HiPo) Identification

**HiPo Criteria (CLC Model):**

| Dimension | Indicators | Assessment Method |
|-----------|-----------|------------------|
| **Ability** | Learning agility, cognitive ability, breadth of skills | Psychometric tests, 360 feedback |
| **Aspiration** | Desire for senior leadership, drive, ambition | Career conversation, self-assessment |
| **Engagement** | Commitment to organization, discretionary effort | eNPS, stay interview, retention risk |

**HiPo Identification Checklist:**

| Criterion | Evidence | Score (1-5) |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Consistently high performance (2+ years) | Performance ratings | /5 |
| Demonstrates behaviors of next level | 360 feedback, manager assessment | /5 |
| Learning agility (adapts to new situations) | Examples, assessments | /5 |
| Leadership presence and influence | Peer feedback, project outcomes | /5 |
| Aspiration for growth and leadership | Career conversation | /5 |
| Engagement and retention likelihood | eNPS, stay interview | /5 |
| **Total** | | **/30** |

- Score 25-30: Confirmed HiPo (top 5-10%)
- Score 20-24: Potential HiPo (monitor, develop)
- Score <20: Valued contributor but not HiPo (different development path)

### Step 5: Leadership Pipeline (Charan)

**6 Leadership Transitions:**

| Transition | From | To | Key Shift | Development Focus |
|-----------|------|-----|-----------|------------------|
| **Passage 1** | Managing Self | Managing Others | Time: from doing to delegating | Delegation, coaching, 1:1s |
| **Passage 2** | Managing Others | Managing Managers | Value: developing managers, not doing | Manager development, alignment |
| **Passage 3** | Managing Managers | Functional Manager | Strategic thinking for function | Cross-functional collaboration |
| **Passage 4** | Functional Manager | Business Manager | P&L responsibility, integration | Business acumen, trade-offs |
| **Passage 5** | Business Manager | Group Manager | Portfolio management | Capital allocation, M&A |
| **Passage 6** | Group Manager | Enterprise Manager | Vision, external stakeholders | Board governance, strategy |

### Step 7: Talent Review Meeting

**Annual Talent Review Agenda (2-4 hours):**
1. Organization health overview (headcount, turnover, diversity)
2. 9-Box placement review (all managers present evidence)
3. HiPo confirmation and development plans
4. Succession map review (critical role coverage)
5. Flight risk assessment (who might leave)
6. Development investment decisions (budget allocation)
7. Action items and owners

## Red Flags

Stop and warn the user if:
- Critical roles with zero identified successors
- HiPo list unchanged for 3+ years (stagnation or wrong criteria)
- No development plans for identified successors (succession in name only)
- Emergency succession plan not updated in 12+ months
- 100% of successors are external candidates (internal development failure)
- No diversity in succession pipeline
- Key person risk score high with no mitigation plan
- Talent review meeting not conducted annually
- Succession planning only for C-suite (should cover all critical roles)
- Identified successors unaware they are in the pipeline (no transparency)
- No budget allocated for HiPo development programs
