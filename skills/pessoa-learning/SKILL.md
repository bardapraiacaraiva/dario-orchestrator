## Frameworks & References

- **Kirkpatrick** — 4 Levels of training evaluation (Reaction, Learning, Behavior, Results)
- **ADDIE** — Instructional design (Analysis, Design, Develop, Implement, Evaluate)
- **70:20:10** — Learning model (70% on-the-job, 20% social, 10% formal)
- **Bloom's Taxonomy** — Cognitive learning levels (Remember through Create)
- **Josh Bersin** — Skills-based organization, learning in the flow of work
- **Codigo do Trabalho (PT)** — 40 hours/year mandatory training (Art. 131)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Skill Gap Analysis

**Company Skills Matrix:**

| Skill | Required Level | Current Level | Gap | Priority | Employee(s) |
|-------|---------------|---------------|-----|----------|-------------|
| [Skill 1] | Advanced | Intermediate | 1 level | High | [Names] |
| [Skill 2] | Expert | Basic | 2 levels | Critical | [Names] |
| [Skill 3] | Intermediate | Intermediate | 0 | OK | [Names] |

**Proficiency Levels:**
- **Beginner** (1): Knows the basics, needs supervision
- **Intermediate** (2): Can work independently on standard tasks
- **Advanced** (3): Handles complex situations, can teach others
- **Expert** (4): Thought leader, designs solutions, strategic application

**Gap Identification Methods:**
1. Manager assessment (fastest)
2. Self-assessment + manager validation
3. Skills test or certification exam
4. 360 feedback competency data (from `pessoa-performance`)
5. Job requirements vs. current capability audit

| Phase | Activities | Output |
|-------|-----------|--------|

| **Develop** | Create materials, slides, exercises, case studies | Training materials |

| **Evaluate** | Measure effectiveness (Kirkpatrick 4 levels) | Evaluation report |

**Learning Objectives (Bloom's):**

**Knowledge Transfer Methods:**
1. **Document**: Write SOPs, guides, playbooks
2. **Record**: Loom videos of key processes
3. **Pair**: Shadow sessions for critical knowledge
4. **Teach**: "Teach-back" sessions (present to team)
5. **Mentoring**: Formal mentoring program for tacit knowledge

### Step 7: Learning Paths by Role

**Example Learning Path: New Manager**

| Month | Focus | Activities | Assessment |
|-------|-------|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | Management fundamentals | Book: "First Time Manager" + Workshop | Quiz |
| 2 | Feedback & 1:1s | Radical Candor workshop + practice | Role play |
| 3 | Performance management | OKR training + first cycle | First OKR set |
| 4 | Hiring & interviewing | Structured interview training | Observed interview |
| 5 | Team dynamics | Lencioni workshop + team assessment | Team survey |
| 6 | Leadership presence | Communication workshop + 360 feedback | 360 results |

### Step 8: Training ROI Measurement (Kirkpatrick)

| Level | What to Measure | Method | When |
|-------|----------------|--------|------|
| **1: Reaction** | Did participants enjoy and find it useful? | Post-training survey (NPS) | Immediately after |
| **2: Learning** | Did they learn the content? | Test, quiz, demonstration | End of training |
| **3: Behavior** | Are they applying it on the job? | Manager observation, 30-day check-in | 30-90 days after |
| **4: Results** | Did it impact business outcomes? | KPI comparison pre/post training | 3-6 months after |

**Training ROI Formula:**
ROI = (Benefits from training - Cost of training) / Cost of training x 100

### Step 9: PT Legal Requirements

| Requirement | Detail | Reference |
|------------|--------|-----------|
| **40 hours/year** | Minimum training per employee | Art. 131 CT |
| **Training plan** | Annual plan, consulted with workers' committee | Art. 131 CT |
| **Training records** | Must document all training delivered | Art. 131 CT |
| **Professional training** | At least 50% must be certified training | Art. 131 CT |
| **Credit accumulation** | Unused hours carry over (max 3 years, cap 70h) | Art. 131 CT |

## Red Flags

Stop and warn the user if:
- Below 40 hours/year training per employee (PT legal non-compliance)
- No training records maintained (audit risk)
- Training not aligned with business needs (waste of budget)
- No skill gap analysis done (training without purpose)
- 100% formal training, 0% on-the-job (70:20:10 imbalance)
- No evaluation of training effectiveness (no ROI visibility)
- Key person risk with no knowledge transfer plan
- Certifications expiring without renewal plan
- Training budget cut to zero (short-term saving, long-term cost)
- No individual development plans for employees
- Manager resistance to releasing staff for training

## 7. Status checklist per data point (Gate 7)

Every numeric value, name, or factual claim in **PESSOA Learning & Development** output must carry an EXPLICIT label so the reader knows what to trust as-is and what needs verification before action.

- 🔵 **verified** — confirmed from a primary source (file, audit log, session memory, RAG hit, screen-shared confirmation)
- 🟡 **assumed** — plausible default that needs client/owner confirmation before being acted on
- 🟢 **projection** — estimate by design (timeline, cost, throughput, conversion); not verifiable today

**Why this gate exists:** outputs that mix verified facts with assumptions and projections without flagging them produce false certainty. The reader either over-trusts (acts on a guess) or under-trusts (ignores even the verified parts). Explicit labels restore signal.

**How to apply in PESSOA Learning & Development:**

1. After drafting the deliverable, scan it for every concrete claim (number, name, date, metric, status, recommendation).
2. Attach one of the three labels inline; if you can't pick a label confidently, the claim isn't ready to ship.
3. Add a short citation in parentheses for 🔵 items (file path, source, dashboard) and a short condition for 🟡 / 🟢 items (what would confirm or refute it).
4. End the deliverable with a 1-line summary of how many items in each category, e.g. `Status mix: 8 🔵 · 3 🟡 · 2 🟢`.

❌ **NOT delivery-ready:**

```
Conversion rate is 18%. CAC is R$ 420. We will hit 1k MAU in Q3.
```

✅ **Delivery-ready:**

```
- Conversion rate: 18% 🔵 verified (Mixpanel funnel report 2026-05-19, n=1,242 sessions)
- CAC: R$ 420 🟡 assumed (calculated from May spend ÷ May customers; CFO has not signed off yet)
- 1k MAU in Q3 🟢 projection (linear extrapolation of last 8 weeks; assumes no churn spike)

Status mix: 1 🔵 · 1 🟡 · 1 🟢
```

**Ship checklist post-cliente-sync:**
- [ ] All 🟡 items confirmed (or downgraded to 🟢 / dropped)
- [ ] All 🔵 citations actually exist (no broken file paths, no imagined sources)
- [ ] All 🟢 projections labeled as such to the client — never presented as commitments
