# ORACULO-PAPER-READING-EXTRACTION

## 3-pass approach (Keshav)
1. **Scan (5-10 min):** title, abstract, conclusions, figures
2. **Read (1h):** detailed, ignore proofs/details
3. **Re-implement (4-5h):** understand deeply

## Stack
- **Semantic Scholar** — paper search + AI summaries
- **Elicit** — AI research assistant
- **Consensus.app** — AI search papers + claim extraction
- **Connected Papers** — citation graph visualization
- **Research Rabbit** — literature graph exploration
- **Zotero + Better Notes** — reference management
- **Notion AI / Reflect** — note synthesis

## Templates
1. Paper reading template (Three-pass output)
2. Literature review matrix
3. Citation tracking (forward + backward)
4. Annotation system
5. Synthesis report
6. Reading group facilitation
