# KIRION-LEASING-STRATEGY

## Tipos lease
- **Gross lease:** landlord paga taxes/insurance/maintenance
- **Net lease (single net):** tenant paga 1 expense category
- **Double net (NN):** tenant paga 2 (taxes + insurance)
- **Triple net (NNN):** tenant paga 3 (taxes + insurance + maintenance) — common commercial
- **Absolute NNN:** tenant paga everything including capex
- **Percentage lease:** % of revenue (retail)
- **Modified gross:** mix
- **Build-to-Suit:** custom for single tenant

## Conceitos chave
- **Effective rent:** stated rent - concessions - amortized TIs
- **Free rent (rent abatement):** 1-12 meses commonly
- **TI allowance:** Tenant Improvement (R$/m²)
- **Escalations:** annual increases (% or inflation-indexed)
- **Caps:** maximum increase per year
- **Renewal options:** at fixed price ou market rate

## BR/PT specifics
- **BR:** Lei 8.245/91 (Inquilinato) — residencial + comercial
- **PT:** NRAU (Novo Regime Arrendamento Urbano) Lei 6/2006
- **Reajuste BR:** IGPM/IPCA tradicional, índice IVAR (novo)
- **Reajuste PT:** coeficiente INE anual
