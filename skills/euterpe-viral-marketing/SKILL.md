# EUTERPE-VIRAL-MARKETING

## K-factor formula
```
K = (Invites per user) × (Conversion rate per invite)
```
- K = 1: linear growth
- K > 1: viral (exponential)
- K < 1: sub-viral (needs paid acquisition)

## Tipos viral loops
- **Word-of-mouth (intrinsic):** users naturally share (Slack)
- **Incentivized:** "$10 each" (Dropbox, Robinhood)
- **Product:** sharing IS the product (WhatsApp, Zoom)
- **Content:** UGC drives discovery (TikTok, Instagram)
- **Embedded:** social proof shown (LinkedIn endorsements)
