# DARIO PR — Public Relations & Communications

## When to activate

- Client launching something newsworthy
- Press release needed
- Media outreach campaign
- Crisis communication (negative press, social media incident)
- Award/prize submission
- Spokesperson preparation for interview
- Media kit creation
- Thought leadership pitch to publications

## Modules

### 1. Press Release Generator

**Inverted pyramid structure (standard PR format):**

```markdown
PRESS RELEASE — FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
[or: EMBARGO UNTIL [date] [time] [timezone]]

[HEADLINE — Title Case, Max 100 chars, Active Voice, Newsworthy]

[SUB-HEADLINE — 1 line that adds context]

[CITY], [DATE] — [Opening paragraph: WHO did WHAT, WHEN, WHERE, WHY.
This paragraph must contain the complete story in 3-4 sentences.
A journalist who reads only this paragraph has the full picture.]

[Second paragraph: Context, background, why this matters.
Include a relevant data point or market context.]

"[Quote from CEO/founder/spokesperson. Make it sound human, not corporate.
The quote should add perspective the facts alone don't convey.]"
— [Name], [Title], [Company]

[Third paragraph: Details, features, availability, technical specs if relevant.
This is where you expand on the what and how.]

[Optional: Second quote from partner, client, or industry expert.]

[Boilerplate — About [Company]:
Standard 3-4 sentence company description. Include:
- What the company does (1 sentence)
- Key achievement or differentiator (1 sentence)
- Founded [year], based in [city] (1 sentence)
- Website URL]

###

MEDIA CONTACT:
[Name]
[Title]
[Email]
[Phone]
[Website]
```

**PT-specific considerations:**
- Portuguese media prefers PT language releases (not EN)
- Include LUSA (agencia de noticias portuguesa) in distribution
- Embargo times respect Lisbon timezone (WET/WEST)
- For tech news: Sapo TEK, Dinheiro Vivo, ECO, Observador Tech
- For business: Jornal de Negocios, Expresso, Publico Economia

### 2. Media Outreach

**Journalist pitch template:**

```
Subject: [Specific, relevant, not clickbait — e.g., "Restaurante em Cascais lanca conceito fusao mar+fogo"]

Ola [Nome do jornalista],

[1 sentence: why this is relevant to THEM specifically — reference a recent article they wrote]

[2-3 sentences: the news/story, with the hook that makes it interesting]

[1 sentence: what's available — interview, photos, data, exclusive access]

[Sign-off with contact info]
```

### 3. Crisis Communication

**Crisis response framework (Golden Hour):**

```
MINUTE 0-15: ASSESS
- What happened? (facts only, no speculation)
- Who is affected? (clients, users, public)
- What's the source? (social media, press, internal)
- Severity: [1-critical | 2-serious | 3-moderate | 4-minor]

MINUTE 15-60: PREPARE
- Draft holding statement (acknowledges, doesn't speculate)
- Identify spokesperson
- Brief legal (if applicable)
- Prepare internal FAQ for team

HOUR 1: RESPOND
- Publish holding statement
- Respond to direct inquiries
- Monitor sentiment

HOUR 2-24: MANAGE
- Detailed response with facts
- Direct communication to affected parties
- Media Q&A if needed

DAY 2-7: RECOVER
- Follow-up actions completed
- Lessons learned
- Process changes to prevent recurrence
```

**Holding statement template:**
```
"Temos conhecimento de [situacao]. Estamos a investigar e tomaremos 
as medidas necessarias. A [seguranca/privacidade/satisfacao] dos nossos 
[clientes/utilizadores] e a nossa prioridade. Partilharemos mais 
informacao assim que a tivermos."
```

### 4. Award Submission

```markdown
## Award Submission — [Award Name]

### Award Details
- Organization: [who gives the award]
- Category: [which category]
- Deadline: [date]
- Fee: [if applicable]
- Format: [requirements — word count, images, video]

### Submission Draft

**Project Overview** (100 words)
[What we did, for whom, and why it matters]

**Challenge** (150 words)
[What was the problem/brief/constraint]

**Solution** (200 words)
[What we created and why this approach]

**Results** (150 words)
[Measurable outcomes: traffic +X%, revenue +Y%, awards won, etc.]

**Innovation** (100 words)
[What makes this different from typical work in this category]

### Supporting Materials
- [ ] High-res images (300dpi, credits)
- [ ] Before/after screenshots
- [ ] Client testimonial
- [ ] Video case study (if required)
- [ ] Metrics dashboard screenshot
```

**PT awards to consider:**
- Premios Eficacia (marketing effectiveness)
- Premios Criatividade (Clube de Criativos de Portugal)
- European Search Awards (SEO/SEM)
- Awwwards / CSS Design Awards (web design)
- Google Premier Partner Awards

- "Se olharmos para os dados..."
- "Na nossa experiencia..."

## Red Flags

- NEVER publish press release without client approval
- NEVER respond to crisis without legal review (if liability involved)
- NEVER fabricate quotes — always get spokesperson sign-off
- NEVER distribute to media without checking embargo terms
- Crisis: speed matters but accuracy matters more — don't guess facts
