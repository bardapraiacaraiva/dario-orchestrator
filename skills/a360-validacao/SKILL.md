# A360 Idea Validation

## Triggers

Activate this skill when the user says any of:
- "validate idea", "validar ideia", "idea validation"
- "smoke test", "landing page test", "teste de conceito"
- "pre-sale", "pre-venda", "waitlist"
- "minimum viable offer", "MVP test"
- "should I build this?", "devo construir isto?"
- "go or no-go", "vale a pena avançar?"
- Any request to test a business idea before building the full product

## Frameworks & References

- **Eric Ries** (Lean Startup) — MVP, validated learning, build-measure-learn loop, innovation accounting
- **Rob Fitzpatrick** (The Mom Test) — how to talk to customers without leading questions

## Workflow

### Step 1: Validation Readiness Check
Before testing, confirm the user has:
- [ ] Defined niche (from a360-nicho or equivalent)
- [ ] Built customer avatar (from a360-avatar or equivalent)
- [ ] Clear product/service concept (1-2 sentences)
- [ ] Hypothesis to test (what assumption needs proof?)

Define the **core hypothesis**:
"[TARGET CUSTOMER] will pay [PRICE] for [OFFER] because it solves [PAIN]."

### Step 2: Choose Validation Method

Select based on budget, time, and business type:

| Method | Cost | Time | Evidence Strength | Best For |
|--------|------|------|-------------------|----------|
| **Mom Test Interviews** | Free | 1-2 weeks | Medium | Early-stage, any idea |
| **Smoke Test Landing Page** | $50-200 | 3-5 days | High | Digital products, SaaS |
| **Waitlist / Email Capture** | $50-100 | 1-2 weeks | Medium-High | Pre-launch products |
| **Pre-Sale / Founding Members** | Free | 1-2 weeks | Very High | Services, courses, communities |
| **Concierge MVP** | Free | 2-4 weeks | Very High | Services, custom solutions |
| **Wizard of Oz** | $100-500 | 1-2 weeks | High | Tech products (fake automation) |
| **Crowdfunding** | $200-500 | 30-60 days | Very High | Physical products |
| **Facebook Group Test** | Free | 1-2 weeks | Medium | Community-based, info products |
| **Paid Ad Test** | $200-500 | 5-7 days | High | Any product with clear offer |

### Step 3: Mom Test Interview Guide (Fitzpatrick)

**Rules:**
- Never ask "would you buy this?" (everyone says yes)
- Ask about their life, not your idea
- Look for BEHAVIOR, not opinions
- Talk less, listen more

**Questions:**
1. "Tell me about the last time you experienced [PROBLEM]."
2. "What did you do to try to solve it?"
3. "What was the hardest part about that?"
4. "How much time/money did that cost you?"
5. "Have you looked for a solution? What did you find?"
6. "What would the perfect solution look like?"
7. "If this existed right now, what would stop you from using it?"

**Target: 15-20 interviews**. If 10+ people describe the same pain with intensity, you have signal.

### Step 4: Smoke Test Landing Page Blueprint

**Page structure:**
1. **Headline**: [Dream outcome] without [pain/sacrifice]
2. **Sub-headline**: For [avatar] who wants [desire]
3. **Problem agitation**: 3 pain points that resonate
4. **Solution introduction**: Your unique mechanism
5. **Social proof placeholder**: "Join X others who..."
6. **CTA**: Email capture, waitlist, or pre-order button
7. **Urgency**: Founding member pricing, limited spots

**Traffic sources for testing:**
- Facebook/Instagram ads: $5-10/day, 7 days = $35-70
- Reddit/forum posts (organic): Free, 7-14 days
- Personal network outreach: Free, immediate
- Google Ads (if search intent exists): $10-20/day, 7 days

**Metrics to track:**
| Metric | Target | Signal |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Landing page conversion rate | >10% | Strong interest |
| CPC (paid traffic) | <$2 | Audience is reachable |
| Email open rate | >40% | Engaged audience |
| Pre-sale conversion | >2% of visitors | Real demand |
| Waitlist size in 7 days | >100 | Market pull |

### Step 5: Pre-Sale / Founding Member Test

The strongest validation: ask people to pay before you build.

**Founding Member Offer:**
- "I'm creating [PRODUCT] and looking for 10 founding members"
- Founding member price: 50-70% off future price
- Extra access: direct input into product, lifetime updates
- Guarantee: full refund if not delivered by [DATE]

**Channels for pre-selling:**
1. Direct outreach to avatar matches (10-20 personal messages)
2. Email list (if any exists)
3. Social media announcement
4. Community posts (where avatar congregates)

**Success criteria:**
- 3-5 paying customers = validated (for high-ticket)
- 10-20 paying customers = validated (for mid-ticket)
- 50+ paying customers = validated (for low-ticket)

### Step 6: Concierge MVP (Ries)

Deliver the service manually before automating:
- No product needed, just your time and expertise
- Charge real money (even if discounted)
- Document every step (this becomes your product spec)
- Measure customer satisfaction and willingness to continue

### Step 7: Paid Ad Validation Sprint

7-day sprint with $100-300 budget:

**Day 1-2:** Launch 3-5 ad variants targeting avatar
**Day 3-4:** Kill underperformers, double down on winners
**Day 5-7:** Optimize and measure final metrics

**Ad variants to test:**
- Pain-focused headline vs desire-focused headline
- Short copy vs long copy
- Image vs video (if applicable)
- Different CTA (waitlist vs pre-order vs learn more)

### Step 8: Validation Scorecard

Score each dimension with evidence:

| Criterion | Weight | Score (1-10) | Evidence | Weighted |
|-----------|--------|-------------|----------|----------|
| **Pain confirmed** (interviews/forums) | 20% | /10 | [source] | |
| **Willingness to pay** (pre-sales/surveys) | 25% | /10 | [source] | |
| **Landing page conversion** | 15% | /10 | [data] | |
| **Traffic availability** (reachable audience) | 15% | /10 | [data] | |
| **Competitive advantage** (differentiation) | 10% | /10 | [analysis] | |
| **Speed to deliver** (can you ship in 30 days?) | 10% | /10 | [assessment] | |
| **Personal conviction** (founder-market fit) | 5% | /10 | [honest answer] | |
| **TOTAL** | 100% | | | **/100** |

### Step 9: Go / No-Go Decision

| Score | Decision | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 80-100 | **GO** — Full speed ahead | Proceed to a360-oferta + a360-modelo |
| 65-79 | **CONDITIONAL GO** — Proceed with caution | Refine offer, retest one weak area |
| 50-64 | **PIVOT** — Adjust significantly | Change avatar, pricing, or delivery model |
| 0-49 | **NO-GO** — Kill this idea | Return to a360-nicho for a new niche |

## Red Flags

Stop and warn the user if:
- User wants to skip validation and "just build it" (Lean Startup anti-pattern)
- All validation is based on friends/family feedback (Mom Test violation)
- Zero people agreed to pay anything (fatal signal)
- User is sinking >$500 into validation without any positive signal
- Landing page conversion is below 3% after 500+ visitors
- Pre-sale attempts yield zero conversions after 50+ qualified prospects
- User is changing the offer every day instead of testing one thing properly
- "Everyone loves the idea but nobody will pay" (classic false positive)

## Handoff

After validation:
- **GO**: Route to `a360-oferta` (build the offer) + `a360-modelo` (design business model)
- **CONDITIONAL GO**: Refine and re-validate specific elements
- **PIVOT**: Route back to `a360-nicho` or `a360-avatar` with new hypotheses
- **NO-GO**: Route to `a360-nicho` for a new market
- Save output to Obsidian: `05 - Claude - IA/Outputs/YYYY-MM-DD - A360 - Validation - [IdeaName].md`
