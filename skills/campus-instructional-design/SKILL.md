# CAMPUS-INSTRUCTIONAL-DESIGN

## Quando usar
- Design de curso novo (online ou presencial)
- Refactor de curso existente (baixa retention)
- Microlearning curriculum
- Corporate training program
- BNCC-aligned curriculum

## Modelos
- **ADDIE:** Analyze/Design/Develop/Implement/Evaluate (clássico)
- **SAM (Allen):** Iterative, agile-like
- **Backward Design (Wiggins/McTighe):** outcomes → assessments → activities
- **UDL (CAST):** Universal Design for Learning (acessibilidade)
- **Bloom's Taxonomy (revisada):** Remember/Understand/Apply/Analyze/Evaluate/Create
- **Webb's DOK:** Depth of Knowledge

## Templates
1. ADDIE course design template
2. Backward Design unit plan (UbD)
3. Bloom's Taxonomy aligned objectives
4. UDL accessibility checklist
5. Course map (modules × outcomes × assessments)
6. Skill matrix (knowledge × application × analysis)

## Princípios chave
- **Outcomes first:** o que deve saber/fazer no final
- **Assessment alignment:** avaliação prova outcomes
- **Active learning:** student é protagonista
- **Spaced + interleaved:** evitar massed practice
- **Feedback loops:** rápido e específico
